Day 3

Fateh Sagar + Saheliyon + Monsoon Palace

2026-09-22 Part 3 / 4

Begin around Fateh Sagar and then visit Saheliyon ki Bari in a low-heat window. Keep this day slower and more visual.

By late afternoon, head toward Sajjangarh (Monsoon Palace) for sunset views if weather is clear.

This is your best day for panoramic city shots.
