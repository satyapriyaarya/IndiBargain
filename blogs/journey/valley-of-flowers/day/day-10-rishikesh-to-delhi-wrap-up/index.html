Wrap-Up Day: Return to Delhi + Budget Summary

2026-07-27 Part 10 / 10

Keep buffer time for city-entry traffic and rail/flight transfers. Once settled, document your trip learning while details are fresh.

Typical per-person budget range for this 10-day style plan (excluding luxury upgrades): INR 18,000 to 35,000 depending on transport mode and room sharing.

Best execution window: mid-July to mid-August for flowers, with strict rain preparedness. Your biggest success factors are early starts, one buffer day, and disciplined packing.

Mountain safety note: weather in this circuit can shift from clear to heavy rain within minutes, so keep rain cover, warm layer, and basic medicines in your daypack instead of main luggage. Plan turn-around times in advance and avoid chasing late-hour shots on slippery trails; consistent safe timing gives better multi-day results than one risky extended walk.
