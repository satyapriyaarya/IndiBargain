Day 8

Descent Day: Ghangaria to Govindghat, then Auli/Joshimath

2026-07-25 Part 8 / 10

Start descent after breakfast and keep strides short on wet rock sections. Use trekking poles if available. At Govindghat, stretch, hydrate, and avoid immediate heavy meals.

From Govindghat, continue to Joshimath or Auli depending on weather and stay options. If roads are unstable, stay closer to transit hubs for flexibility.

Use evening for budget check: transport, stays, permits, food, and contingency. This helps your return leg stay controlled.

Mountain safety note: weather in this circuit can shift from clear to heavy rain within minutes, so keep rain cover, warm layer, and basic medicines in your daypack instead of main luggage. Plan turn-around times in advance and avoid chasing late-hour shots on slippery trails; consistent safe timing gives better multi-day results than one risky extended walk.
