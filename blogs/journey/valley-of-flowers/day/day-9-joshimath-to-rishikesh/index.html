Day 9

Return Transit: Joshimath to Rishikesh

2026-07-26 Part 9 / 10

Leave early and keep your driver informed about rest and tea breaks. Mountain fatigue can build silently, so stick to controlled pacing.

Monsoon rule: if a stretch looks risky due to fresh fall debris or heavy runoff, wait it out. Delays are better than rushed decisions on narrow curves.

Reach Rishikesh by evening, take a light walk, and keep next-day plan flexible based on train/bus timing to Delhi.

Mountain safety note: weather in this circuit can shift from clear to heavy rain within minutes, so keep rain cover, warm layer, and basic medicines in your daypack instead of main luggage. Plan turn-around times in advance and avoid chasing late-hour shots on slippery trails; consistent safe timing gives better multi-day results than one risky extended walk.
