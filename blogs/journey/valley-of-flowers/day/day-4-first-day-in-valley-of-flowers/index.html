Day 4

Main Experience: First Day in Valley of Flowers

2026-07-21 Part 4 / 10

Wake up before sunrise and enter as early as gate timings allow. Carry permit documents, water, dry snacks, and one warm layer because weather flips quickly.

Instead of rushing photos at the first meadows, keep walking deeper for quieter sections and broader valley views. Keep 20-30 minute weather breaks and avoid standing near steep loose slopes if rain intensifies.

Return to Ghangaria with daylight left. Backup your photos, dry shoes with newspaper stuffing, and prepare a lean pack for Hemkund tomorrow.

Mountain safety note: weather in this circuit can shift from clear to heavy rain within minutes, so keep rain cover, warm layer, and basic medicines in your daypack instead of main luggage. Plan turn-around times in advance and avoid chasing late-hour shots on slippery trails; consistent safe timing gives better multi-day results than one risky extended walk.
