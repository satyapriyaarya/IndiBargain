Day 3

Trek Start: Govindghat to Ghangaria

2026-07-20 Part 3 / 10

Route flow: Govindghat to Pulna by local transport, then trek to Ghangaria. Keep a slow and steady pace because this day sets your energy for the next two high-value days.

Start by 6:00 AM. Keep one rain layer always accessible, not packed deep. Trek poles are useful on wet stones and descending sections.

At Ghangaria, check weather updates for Valley of Flowers and Hemkund. Dinner should be early, hydration high, and backpack repacked with only essentials for tomorrow's valley exploration.

Mountain safety note: weather in this circuit can shift from clear to heavy rain within minutes, so keep rain cover, warm layer, and basic medicines in your daypack instead of main luggage. Plan turn-around times in advance and avoid chasing late-hour shots on slippery trails; consistent safe timing gives better multi-day results than one risky extended walk.
