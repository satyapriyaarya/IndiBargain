Day 2

Long Drive Day: Rishikesh to Joshimath

2026-07-19 Part 2 / 10

Distance is manageable on paper, but mountain traffic and weather can stretch this day to 9-11 hours. Keep at least 2 extra hours buffer for monsoon delays.

Suggested break points: Devprayag, Rudraprayag, and Karnaprayag for short stops. Eat light and hydrate often. Avoid late-night driving after Pipalkoti in rain.

Stay in Joshimath if rooms are available, else in Govindghat. Charge all devices, keep an offline map downloaded, and pack a small daypack for tomorrow's trek start.

Mountain safety note: weather in this circuit can shift from clear to heavy rain within minutes, so keep rain cover, warm layer, and basic medicines in your daypack instead of main luggage. Plan turn-around times in advance and avoid chasing late-hour shots on slippery trails; consistent safe timing gives better multi-day results than one risky extended walk.
