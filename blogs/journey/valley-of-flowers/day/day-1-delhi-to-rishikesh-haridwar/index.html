Arrival Day: Delhi to Rishikesh or Haridwar

2026-07-18 Part 1 / 10

Best start window: leave Delhi between 4:30 AM and 6:00 AM to avoid NCR traffic and reach Rishikesh by late afternoon. If you are taking a train, Haridwar is usually easier for onward road travel next morning.

Today is all about prep, not speed. Buy poncho, quick-dry trek pants, a rain cover for your bag, and a basic blister kit. Keep 2 passport photos and one government ID copy for quick registrations where needed.

Sleep goal: 7-8 hours. Tomorrow has a long mountain drive and strong roadside curves. Keep medicines for motion sickness, and avoid heavy oily dinner.

Mountain safety note: weather in this circuit can shift from clear to heavy rain within minutes, so keep rain cover, warm layer, and basic medicines in your daypack instead of main luggage. Plan turn-around times in advance and avoid chasing late-hour shots on slippery trails; consistent safe timing gives better multi-day results than one risky extended walk.
