Day 8

Departure Day + Practical Budget Wrap-Up

2026-11-17 Part 8 / 8

Plan your exit city based on onward tickets: Jorhat, Dibrugarh, or Guwahati. Keep at least 4 to 6 hours of transfer buffer on departure day.

Typical shared-travel budget for this route: INR 20,000 to 38,000 per person depending on stay category, safari choice, and private vehicle usage.

Best optimization for next trip: keep one extra day for weather and ferry uncertainty, and add an optional food trail in Guwahati at the start or end.

Trip rhythm tip: keep one fixed check-in routine every evening - confirm next-day transport, charge power bank and camera batteries, refill water, and set out weather-appropriate layers before sleeping. This 10-minute routine prevents missed starts and improves photo light opportunities because you can leave early without rushing.
