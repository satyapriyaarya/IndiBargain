Day 3

Road Transfer: Guwahati to Kaziranga

2026-11-12 Part 3 / 8

Distance is comfortable, but keep buffer for highway traffic and roadwork stretches. Start post-breakfast and plan tea breaks every 2 to 2.5 hours.

Reach Kaziranga (Kohora side preferred for first-timers) by afternoon. Check in, rest, and do a short orientation walk around the local village belt.

Tonight, sleep early. Keep binoculars, ID, camera batteries, and one warm layer ready for sunrise safari tomorrow.

Trip rhythm tip: keep one fixed check-in routine every evening - confirm next-day transport, charge power bank and camera batteries, refill water, and set out weather-appropriate layers before sleeping. This 10-minute routine prevents missed starts and improves photo light opportunities because you can leave early without rushing.
