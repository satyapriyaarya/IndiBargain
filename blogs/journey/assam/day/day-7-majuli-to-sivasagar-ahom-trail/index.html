Day 7

Majuli to Sivasagar: Ahom History Trail

2026-11-16 Part 7 / 8

Return by ferry and continue onward to Sivasagar. Keep your day structured around 2-3 key historical sites instead of trying to cover everything.

Prioritize Rang Ghar, Talatal Ghar, and Shiva Dol, and spend time reading local historical context at each location. This makes the route more meaningful than quick check-ins.

If energy allows, do a short evening walk near nearby tanks and heritage zones before dinner.

Trip rhythm tip: keep one fixed check-in routine every evening - confirm next-day transport, charge power bank and camera batteries, refill water, and set out weather-appropriate layers before sleeping. This 10-minute routine prevents missed starts and improves photo light opportunities because you can leave early without rushing.
