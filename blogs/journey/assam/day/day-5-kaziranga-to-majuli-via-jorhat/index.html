Day 5

Kaziranga to Majuli via Jorhat Ferry

2026-11-14 Part 5 / 8

Leave Kaziranga early and reach the ferry point toward Majuli with sufficient buffer. Ferry schedules can vary by season and river condition, so keep alternate timing in mind.

Once in Majuli, check into a homestay or eco-cottage. Spend the evening walking around village paths and open fields near your stay.

Dinner should be simple and early. Tomorrow is ideal for cultural immersion and slow photography.

Trip rhythm tip: keep one fixed check-in routine every evening - confirm next-day transport, charge power bank and camera batteries, refill water, and set out weather-appropriate layers before sleeping. This 10-minute routine prevents missed starts and improves photo light opportunities because you can leave early without rushing.
