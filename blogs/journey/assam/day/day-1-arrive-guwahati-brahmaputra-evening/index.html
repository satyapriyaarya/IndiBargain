Day 1

Arrival in Guwahati + Brahmaputra Sunset

2026-11-10 Part 1 / 8

Arrive in Guwahati and check into a hotel around Paltan Bazar, Pan Bazaar, or GS Road for easier onward travel. Keep this day intentionally light so your body adjusts after transit.

By late afternoon, head to the Brahmaputra riverfront near Fancy Bazaar/Uzan Bazaar side. If available, take a short evening cruise and watch the sunset over the wide river channel.

Dinner recommendation: try a simple Assamese thali with rice, dal, local greens, and fish tenga. Keep hydration high and sleep early because Day 2 starts with an early temple visit.

Trip rhythm tip: keep one fixed check-in routine every evening - confirm next-day transport, charge power bank and camera batteries, refill water, and set out weather-appropriate layers before sleeping. This 10-minute routine prevents missed starts and improves photo light opportunities because you can leave early without rushing.
