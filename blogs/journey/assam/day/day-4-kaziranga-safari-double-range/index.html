Kaziranga Safari Day (Morning + Afternoon)

2026-11-13 Part 4 / 8

Take an early morning safari in one zone, then keep a second safari in another range later in the day. This improves chances of diverse sightings and better light conditions for photos.

Safari discipline: avoid bright clothes, remain quiet, and follow guide instructions strictly. Carry water and keep camera settings ready before entering the core zone.

Evening can be used for local cultural performances where available. Keep your next-day transfer logistics finalized before dinner.

Trip rhythm tip: keep one fixed check-in routine every evening - confirm next-day transport, charge power bank and camera batteries, refill water, and set out weather-appropriate layers before sleeping. This 10-minute routine prevents missed starts and improves photo light opportunities because you can leave early without rushing.
