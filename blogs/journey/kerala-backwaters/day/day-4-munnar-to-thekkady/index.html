Day 4

Munnar to Thekkady + Spice Trails

2026-08-15 Part 4 / 6

Leave Munnar by late morning and target Thekkady check-in by afternoon. Keep route breaks short and hydration high.

Evening can be spent in spice plantation guided walk or local market exploration.

Tomorrow includes backwater transition, so keep luggage compact and ready.

Kerala route tip: keep one fixed window every evening to confirm next-day pickup timing, expected drive duration, and weather conditions, especially during ghat-road transfers and houseboat check-in days. A small planning check like this reduces stress, prevents missed slots, and gives you better daylight blocks for photography and local food stops.
