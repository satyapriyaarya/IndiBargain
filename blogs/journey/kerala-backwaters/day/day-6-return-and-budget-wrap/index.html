Day 6

Return + Kerala Budget Summary

2026-08-17 Part 6 / 6

Disembark after breakfast and transfer to Kochi/airport/rail based on onward plan. Keep 4-5 hour departure margin for safe routing.

Typical per-person shared budget for this 6-day route: INR 20,000 to 45,000 depending on houseboat type and resort category.

Best season: September to March for comfortable weather balance.

Kerala route tip: keep one fixed window every evening to confirm next-day pickup timing, expected drive duration, and weather conditions, especially during ghat-road transfers and houseboat check-in days. A small planning check like this reduces stress, prevents missed slots, and gives you better daylight blocks for photography and local food stops.
