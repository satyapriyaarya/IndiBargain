Departure + Pink City Budget Wrap

2026-09-28 Part 4 / 4

Keep first half for final purchases and packing. Use fixed departure windows to avoid last-minute road delays.

Typical 4-day budget range: INR 11,000 to 30,000 per person (shared), based on stay category and shopping intensity.

Best season: October to March for comfortable sightseeing.

Jaipur planning tip: keep one flexible 60-90 minute slot every day for traffic delays, extended monument queues, or unplanned market stops. This small buffer helps you maintain a calm pace, protects your golden-hour photography window, and avoids skipping meals or rushing checkout timings.
