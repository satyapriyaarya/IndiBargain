Arrival + Hawa Mahal Side + Bazaar Walk

2026-09-25 Part 1 / 4

Check in around Bani Park or MI Road for balanced movement. Start with Hawa Mahal exterior and nearby lanes in evening light.

Walk through Johari and Bapu Bazaar but keep shopping focused with a list.

Dinner: try dal bati churma or laal maas based on preference.

Jaipur planning tip: keep one flexible 60-90 minute slot every day for traffic delays, extended monument queues, or unplanned market stops. This small buffer helps you maintain a calm pace, protects your golden-hour photography window, and avoids skipping meals or rushing checkout timings.
